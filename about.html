About Me

Financial Data Analyst & Associate Chartered Accountant

I'm a Financial Data Analyst and Associate Chartered Accountant with over 10 years' experience in financial reporting, FP&A, and data analytics across fast‑paced, detail‑driven environments. I specialise in translating complex financial data into clear, actionable insights that support better decision‑making for finance leaders and operational teams.

Based in Andover, Hampshire, I work primarily with Power BI, DAX, SQL, Python and Microsoft Fabric to design robust data models, build intuitive executive dashboards, and automate recurring reporting processes. I'm a Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst (PL300), which validates my expertise in data analysis and visualization. Leveraging my ACA training, I bring a strong grounding in IFRS‑based financial reporting, budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, and cash‑flow management, which allows me to connect the numbers to the underlying business drivers.

My recent projects include building interactive Power BI reports for management accounts and KPI tracking, developing SQL‑driven data models to streamline month‑end reporting, and using Python for scenario analysis and forecasting. I'm particularly interested in roles where I can partner with stakeholders as a Finance Business Partner or FP&A Analyst, helping them understand performance, challenge assumptions, and identify opportunities to improve profitability and resource allocation.

My journey into finance was unconventional. Although I was initially offered a different academic path, I chose to pursue accounting from the ground up—progressing through AAT, to ACCA, and completing my ACA qualification. That experience shaped how I work today: I take ownership, build systems from fundamentals, and focus on outcomes rather than limitations.

Whether it's building a financial reporting system from scratch, streamlining a month-end close, or helping leadership teams understand their financial performance, I bring both the technical skill and the drive to deliver real impact.
