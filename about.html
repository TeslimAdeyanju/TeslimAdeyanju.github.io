About Me

I am Teslim Adeyanju, an ACA Chartered Accountant and Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst (PL-300) with over 13 years of experience across financial reporting, FP&A, and data analytics.

My work sits at the intersection of finance and data. I help organisations move from fragmented numbers to clear, reliable insight that supports confident decision making.

I specialise in budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, and reporting automation, supported by strong technical capability in Power BI, SQL, Python, and Excel. This combination allows me to go beyond traditional reporting and build systems that transform how finance operates.

What I Do

I design and deliver end to end finance analytics solutions, covering the full lifecycle:

  • Data ingestion and transformation using SQL and Python
  • Structured data modelling and validation
  • Interactive Power BI dashboards for performance tracking
  • Forecasting models and scenario analysis
  • Board level reporting and decision support

My focus is simple: Turn complex financial data into clear, actionable insight.
