About

I'm a Chartered Accountant (ACA) with over 13 years of hands-on experience in financial reporting, management accounting, and business finance — now specialising in financial data analytics.

I build end-to-end pipelines that take raw finance data — from NHS trust returns, management accounts, and market feeds — and transform them into clean, audit-ready insight using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

My MSc in Finance & Investment (Distinction, UK) and ACA qualification together give me a rare combination: deep financial rigour and modern data engineering.

I'm currently based in the UK and targeting Finance Analyst, Management Accountant, and Information Analyst roles in the NHS, local government, and civil service.
